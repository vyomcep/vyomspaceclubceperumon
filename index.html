About Us

Founded on April 7, 2023, Vyom Space Club is the premier student-led aerospace and astronomy organization at the College of Engineering Perumon.

Our club serves as a collaborative platform for students across all engineering disciplines to explore space science through technical workshops, research initiatives, and observational sessions.

Our Pillars

Astronomy

Observation sessions and stargazing events using campus equipment.

Aerospace

Workshops on rocketry, satellite design, and propulsion systems.

Research

Collaborative technical projects and student-led paper publications.
